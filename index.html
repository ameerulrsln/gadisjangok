What We Offer

Our Workshops

Small-group sessions designed to slow down, get messy, and make something you're truly proud of.

Most Loved ✨

Batik Painting

Learn the traditional wax-resist technique on fabric. Design your own patterns and leave with a one-of-a-kind wearable piece of art you made yourself.

⏱ 3 hours 👥 Max 10 pax 🌟 All levels
Community event · Free / low cost

Zine Workshop

Cut, paste, draw and collage your way to a mini-magazine. Express yourself across 8 tiny pages — no art experience needed, just curiosity.

⏱ 2 hours 👥 Max 12 pax 🌟 All levels
Community event · Free / low cost

Flower Making

Craft delicate fabric or paper blooms using traditional techniques. Make a beautiful bouquet you can keep forever as a timeless keepsake.

⏱ 2.5 hours 👥 Max 8 pax 🌟 All levels
Community event · Free / low cost

Our Story

Born from a love of making

"We believe every woman holds an artist within — she just needs the right space to bloom."

Gadis Jangok started as a small creative gathering among friends who simply wanted to make pretty things together. What began in a cozy corner has grown into a warm community of makers across Malaysia.

We run workshops that feel like a warm hug — welcoming, unhurried, and full of laughter. All materials are provided. No experience required, just a willing heart.

Est. 2025 Terengganu based Women-led Beginner-friendly
